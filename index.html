About Us

Burhani Engineering is a new name in the commercial kitchen manufacturing industry, established in early 2025. Founded by an industry veteran with years of experience at Hatimi International, the journey began with freelancing and project-based work under other names. But with growing demand, industry knowledge, and a passion for precision, we decided to step forward under our own banner.

At Burhani Engineering, we bring your kitchen vision to life with quality craftsmanship, practical design, and a commitment to reliability.
